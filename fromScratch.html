Why my interest in golf?

I have been playing golf since my freshman year of high school. My dad has been playing for a long time so I decided that I wanted to start playing too. So I joined my high school's girls golf team. I wasn't very good at first and barely played in any matches. But I practiced regularly and improved a lot over time. Eventually I was able to start playing in every match my junior year and almost made it to state. I then become the captain of our team for my senior year and got first in a few matches that year. I now play golf for fun during my free time and I get to keep improving. I have been taking a golf class here at BYU and when I come back from my mission I will have the opportunity to become a TA for the golf class.
